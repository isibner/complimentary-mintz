Welcome to the Mintz quotes repository.

  1. Provide a place to quickly log the craziness that comes out of Max Mintz's mouth on a daily basis.

You can enter quotes here, or tweet with any combination of the following: @MaxMintzzz, @CompMintz, or #MaxMintz. We'll scrape Twitter every couple minutes and add the most recent quotes to the list. Thanks!
